The Journey

Buenos Aires · Palermo
  • Arrive, decompress, settle into the city's rhythm — no agenda

  • Late dinners in the city that doesn't eat before 9 pm

2 nights
Casa de Uco
Mendoza · Uco Valley Vineyards
  • Fly Buenos Aires → Mendoza (Aerolíneas, ~2 hrs) — Andes appear immediately on approach
  • Vineyard living: rows of vines to the horizon, mountain light at every hour
  • One winery tasting per day maximum — this is not a tour, it's a settling in
  • Long lunches with afternoon light falling across the table
  • Spa, silence, and the specific quality of dusk in the high desert
Two nights is lean but meaningful — enough to feel the altitude and the quiet before the birthday journey continues south to the sierras.

  • Fly Mendoza → Córdoba (~1.5 hrs), private transfer to the estancia (~1.5 hrs)

  • Lodge-based: intimate, unhurried, no resort agenda
  • Horseback riding across open land through the Córdoba sierras
  • Long scenic drives, fireplace evenings, the deep quiet of a working estancia
  • September 25: full birthday sequence — see The Day tab
The Mendoza–Córdoba flight is short and morning-friendly. Arrive at the estancia by early afternoon — time to settle in, ride, and be ready for the birthday evening exactly as it should be.

The Day

25 September 2026  ·  Estancia Los Potreros  ·  Córdoba Sierras

The Arrival and the Evening

A short morning flight, then the sierras open up. By afternoon the estancia is yours.

Morning
Last Morning at Casa de Uco
Wake slowly in the vineyard. A light breakfast before the journey. The Andes on the horizon, the vines still. No hurry — the flight is short and the estancia will wait.
Late morning
Mendoza → Córdoba
A short domestic flight — less than ninety minutes. The landscape shifts beneath you: vineyard and altitude giving way to the rolling sierras of Córdoba. The private transfer begins at the airport.
Early afternoon
Arrival at Estancia Los Potreros
The transfer winds into the hills — and then the estancia appears. A fireplace. An open sky. The deep quiet of a place far from everything. This is where the birthday begins in earnest. Fifty, and you have arrived somewhere extraordinary.
Coordinate with the property: arrival time, in-room welcome, and the evening's arrangements confirmed well in advance. The estancia team are exceptional at making moments like this feel effortless.
Afternoon
Settle In — Horseback or Rest
If the light and energy allow: a short horseback ride out across the property before the afternoon gets long. Or simply: unpack slowly, a hot bath, lie down in a room full of good air. Either is exactly right.
Sunset
Champagne with Andean Views
The sierras going golden, then rose, then dark. A glass of champagne already poured. The Andean light at its most dramatic. You are fifty years old, exactly here, on September 25th. Take it in without rushing — the evening is long.
Pre-arrange with the estancia: champagne on ice at the viewing point or terrace. No elaborate setup needed — the landscape does all the work.
Evening
Private Dinner by Fireplace
Candlelit. Intimate, not performative. The fireplace lit, the sierra darkness outside the windows. An exceptional Malbec — the kind that only Argentina produces at altitude. A dinner that knows what it's celebrating without needing to announce it. Just the two of you, the fire, and fifty years arriving at exactly this moment.
Confirm with the estancia team: private dinner setting, chosen wine, birthday dessert if desired. State the preference clearly — intimate and understated, not performative. They will understand.
Later
The Fireplace. The Rest of the Night.
The sierras are completely dark and utterly silent. The fire burns low. Five more nights remain at the estancia — and that is exactly enough time to let this one fully settle in.
